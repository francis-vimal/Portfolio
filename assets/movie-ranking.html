My favorite video games of all time

Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice

The games mechanics really felt like samurai to me. I really like the parry mechanic in this game and visuals are so damn good.

The story of this game will melt your heart. The graphics are intense and brutual.

The realistic world can make us to play this game is more and more. The gameplay physics are realistic, the interaction are on point and straight forward. I'm damn sure you will cry at the ending part of this game.
